Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a simple lunch meal that people of all ages can enjoy. Whether it's eaten alone or with other healthy snacks, it can be the perfect way to satisfy anyone's hunger.

Ingredients

  • peanut butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread of your choice

Steps

  1. Place two slices of bread on plate
  2. Spread peanut butter onto one slice
  3. Spread jelly on other slice
  4. Place both sides together and cut in half
  5. Enjoy!
